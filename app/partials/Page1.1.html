Difference between EOS M and EOS D-SLRs

Function Difference

EOS M

  • Viewfinder: LCD monitor (Live View Display)
  • AF:Phase-difference AF
  • Light meter/control:Imaging area light meter/control)

EOS

  • Viewfinder: Optical Viewfinder
  • Viewfinder: LCD Monitor(Live View Display)
  • Light meter/control: Optical Viewfinder + Light meter/control
