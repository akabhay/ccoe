High-speed noise reduction processing even at high ISO speeds

With earlier EOS Digital cameras , setting Hight ISO Noise Reduction to "strong" would reduce the number of shots that could be captured in a continuous burst. This is because images had to remain in the buffer longer than normal in order to undergo noise processing in multiple sequence.With DIGIC 5+, images undergo the various processes simultaneously. This eliminates the problem of a drop in performance of the maximum continuous shooting burst.Noise processing is also more effective due to more extensive pixel analysis. Shadow color noise and graininess are prevented, contributed to higher ISO speeds and image quality
